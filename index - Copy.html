In folklore,werewolf, or occasionally lycanthrope,is an individual that can shapeshift into a wolf, either purposely or after being placed under a curse or affliction with the transformations occurring on the night of a full moon.A werewolf is a species of legendary creature that can hide its ears and tail. It is a human but also a wolf. Werewolves are, according to some legends, people who morph into vicious, powerful wolves. Others are a mutant combination of human and wolf.
